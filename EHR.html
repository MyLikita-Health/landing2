my likita is an educational Electronic Heath Record (EHR) and learning platform.
designed to encourage students to see the holistic nature of healthcare while
becoming proficient in its technology. the Go EHR includes 700+ customizable
patient cases and activites built around the diverse and relistic human stories
healthcare professionals see every day.

Used in all healthcare disciplines, Go is fully interprofessional and can be used
both within and between programs, in simulation, classroom, lab, practice, or
for clinicals.
